Project Borinquena

Project Borinquena (Project Puerto Rico) is an informative site to guide those to help Puerto Rico in their time of need.

Goals

Education Goal

All Colleges, Universities, and Schools remain (or reopen) open to include STEAM courses and to never have to shut their doors again because of lack of funding.

Medical Goal

Hospitals and Clinics remain open (or reopen) to always have their doors open. To maintain a constant flow of patient and patient treatment.

Education Solution

Donate to the colleges and universities of Puerto Rico. Donate to local high schools, middle schools, and elementary schools to maintain their doors open to educate the future of Puerto Rico.

Medical Solution

Donate to Regional/Local Hospitals and Clinics to Maintain funds for patient medication and treatments, as well as employee salaries. Volunteer or Apply for jobs at hospitals/clinics by joining the staff and increasing the amount of treated patients.
